WHAT IS E-RUPEE (CBDC)

In simple words, the e-Rupee (e₹) is the digital form of the Indian rupee notes stored in a digital wallet.
That’s why it’s commonly referred to as Digital Rupee. e-Rupee is developed on blockchain
technology and controlled by the Indian authority and Reserve Bank of India. Its also called CBDC which stands for ‘Central Bank Digital Currency’.
